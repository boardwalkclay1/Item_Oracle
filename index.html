How Item Oracle works

1. Scan for items

  1. Turn on Bluetooth on your computer or phone.
  2. Click Scan for items.
  3. Select one or more items from the list that appears.

2. See items on the radar

  1. Connected items appear as glowing dots around you.
  2. The distance in feet is an estimate based on Bluetooth signal or your item’s data.
  3. Click a dot or list entry to see details.

3. Rename and organize

  1. Use the rename button to give items clear names, like “Keys” or “Backpack”.
  2. Add categories like “Everyday Carry” or “Travel” and assign items to them.
  3. The radar and list can be filtered by category.

4. Approvals, alerts, and sound

  1. Turn on notifications to allow alerts from Item Oracle.
  2. Turn on sound alerts if you want tones when items move away or come back.
  3. You can change these choices at any time.
Item Oracle runs completely in your browser using Bluetooth. No WiFi is required.
Use in Chrome or Edge, over HTTPS or localhost, with Bluetooth, notifications, and sound enabled if you choose.
